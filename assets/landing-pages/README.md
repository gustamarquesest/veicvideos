# Assets da landing de sites e landing pages

Substitua os placeholders da `landing-pages.html` por estes arquivos quando as imagens reais estiverem prontas.

## Mockups

- `showcase-principal.webp` - 1600x1200 - mockup principal da vitrine
- `showcase-mobile-01.webp` - 900x1600 - mockup vertical de landing page
- `showcase-mobile-02.webp` - 900x1600 - segundo mockup vertical
- `showcase-devices.webp` - 1600x1000 - desktop, tablet e celular
- `showcase-site-institucional.webp` - 1600x1000 - exemplo de site institucional
- `og-landing-pages.webp` - 1200x630 - imagem Open Graph opcional
